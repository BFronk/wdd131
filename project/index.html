Start playing games!!
So you want to start playing Board and card games more? Good for you!! Playing board and card games is a great way to socialize and have fun especially if you're not good at making conversation like me. This website will help you to get started and make it an incredible experience in doing so.
HOW TO START: It is a good Idea to start with smaller and simpler games that will be easy to understand and anyone could learn in a few minutes. Then, after you get going and you have friends who want to play more complex games, go for it!
COMPETITION: Competition is the main way that plating these games will become incredibly fun(especially if youre able to develop some competitive 1 on 1 with your crush, Pro tip) If you feel an inkling to keep track of how many times you and your friends have won and lost, That is a great idea and I recommend it! if you're able to sneak it up in conversation as well bonus points to you.
CONSISTENCY: Another thing that will help you on yoour quest to playing these games and getting all of the benefits is consistently playing them and develoiping a schedule. I recommend trying to do it weekly if at all possible.
