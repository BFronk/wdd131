10.Intimidator 305-305ft

Our first coaster is I-305. Known for it's grueling intensity! Located at Kings dominion and sporting a sleek red track this thing is a beast.

9.Leviathan-306ft

Next up is Leviathan Located at Canada's Wonderland. It is the tallest coaster in Canada. Crazy that all the coasters on this list are giga coasters ehich are 300+ feet

8.Tormentas Rampaging Run-309ft

This one is a bit of a fun one it is not built yet is scheduled to be completed in 2026. It will be the worlds tallest, fastest and longest dive coaster and will have 5 inversions, tied for the record.

7.Millenium Force-311ft

Millenium Force was the first coaster to ever break 300 feet! It is regarded as one of the best coasters in the world and is very forceful

6.Steel Dragon 2000 318ft

Located in Japan at Nagashima Spa Land. It opened as the longest roller coaster in the world at a staggering 8133 feet! It opened as the tallest and fastest in the world as well, which records have been broken.

5.Fury 325ft

this coaster seems to be at the top five of everyone's list who has been on it. It is glass, smooth, and a great experience with some good forces. Located at Carowinds

4.Red Force 367ft

The next few coasters are quite short with a massive high point in the ride. Red Force is located in Spain at ferrari world. It is a extremely powerful launch into a massive top hat.

3.Superman-Escape From Krypton 415ft

This is a bit of a different ride experience you are launched backwards but still pulled up the rest of the vertical spike. This one isn't talked about much considering its staggering height.

2.Top Thrill 2 420ft

TT2 has a long history. Formerly top thrill dragster, with less to offer. Now there's a swing launch and backward spike added into the ride. Weaker launch but the spike is incredible

Honerable mention:Kingda Ka 456 ft

Kingda Ka opened as the tallest and fastest roller coaster in 2005. Earlier this year (2025) It got destoyed to make way for more attractions. But it held the record for two decades as the tallest coaster in the world.

1.Falcons Flight 600+ft

Set to demolish the hieght speed and length world records. 600+ feet, almost 2 1/2 miles long, and a drop of a cliff to 150 MPH to set the speed WR. track is built and testing has begun!
