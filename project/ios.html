iPhoneOS 1: That's right, the first versions of the iPhones software were called iPhoneOS instead of the IOS we know today. The software shipped on the original iPhone was primitive compared to our day but dealt with a brand new input device being the human finger. You could now scroll, pinch and touch your content directly in a way never done before.

New features came to Mail, Contacts, maps, calculator, and general setting including a scientific calculator and the ability to turn Wi-Fi on while in Airplane mode.
